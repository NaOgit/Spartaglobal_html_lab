Hakuna Matata

Orginal Sountrack from the movie, "The Lion King".
"Hakuna Matata" is a song based on Timon and Pumbaa's catchphrase, which means 'no worries'. The song is characterised in
simple 4/4 time upbeat pace. Very catchy.
It was composed by Elton John, with lyrics by Tim Rice. The song features a voice of a meerkat (Timon, voiced by Nathan Lane),
a voice of a warthog (Pumbaa, voiced by Ernie Sabella), and a voice of a lion (Simba, voiced by Jason Weaver and Joseph WIlliams).
